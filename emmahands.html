Overview

Tough Titties Designs was created and founded by Emma Hands as a brand specializing in creating light-hearted designed items that promote female empowerment and raise awareness for breast cancer. Heavily emphasizing the inclusion of diverse voices and celebration of individual strengths.

Hands' struggles with social media, leading to a lack of social media presence, making it difficult to keep up with trends and relate to her target market. She wants to truly understand her target audience to pinpoint how to capture her target market and increase her conversion to sales.

Empathy

After interviewing Hands, several pain points were identified which led to insightful design directions, including:

  • Defining the Distinction Between Brand and Identity
  • Bridging the Physical and Digital Touchpoints
  • Building Community Connections to the Audience

User Research

To conduct further research, we analyzed the Etsy and Instagram platforms, and had conversations with people who were interested in Hands' causes, as well as people who were interested in her products. The findings are as follows:

  • Around 50% of Hands' customers write purchase notes to her during online shopping. Customers like to share their experiences and moivations on Etsy reviews
  • There is a lack of community on Instagram
  • Seeing the different breast shapes on products is extremely important in representation, as it helps with addressing what is not commonly shown, indicating that the supporters are not alone.

This information shows that people do have stories to tell and want to share their experiences. However, there exists a disconnect and lack of interaction between the supporters in the current space of Tough Titties Designs.

How Might We

help customers who support women empowerment better connect with the business by building a sense of community in a space that has a desire of belonging and interactions?

Proposal

The proposed design includes an interactive space where customers can connect with one another and share personal stories and experiences. The solution uses breast icons to represent individuals, and displays written stories when the icons are selected.

Art Direction

The design aims to maintain a fun and light-hearted approach, inspired by Hands' brand identity, despite the serious problem space. Adopting Tough Titties Designs' simple visual design and branding colors, and added customizable boob shapes to enhance their existing variety.

User Flow

Card

Having a card be added to every purchase serves as a bridge between physical, in-real-life products,to the digital media.

Through user testing, it is found that having a clear purpose of card is important, thus going through many iterations. To ensure a strong call to action, a simple and minimalistic layout that is not overloaded with information is used, leaving a mystery to attract people to explore more.

Onboarding

This page would not only serve as instructions on how to use the site, but also an introduction as to what the purpose is about.

Gallery

The gallery would display 10 random pairs of breasts. This is meant to resemble real life gathering experience in which you never know who you will meet. By using the shuffle button, users explore a new set of stories. There is also an option to filter the search in order to help users to quickly find stories that they resonate with.

Create Your Own Story

To prevent abuse and unwanted contents from being posted, an access code must be entered. This one time access code can be found on the card recieved with each purchase.

Customizing

Users are able to express themselves through creating their own identity that they resonate with.

View Story

Users can connect with other others through a diverse range of stories, knowing that they are not alone and have a way to support others. Stamps are used to allow engagement and have a more intament experience.
